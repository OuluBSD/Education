# Intro Presentation: Music Production Journey

## Slide 1 — History of Music Production: From Analog to Digital
- 1950s-60s: Multi-track tape recording (4-track, then 8-track)
- 1970s-80s: Rise of professional recording studios with large mixing consoles
- 1980s: Introduction of MIDI protocol, allowing instruments to communicate
- 1990s: Early digital audio workstations (Pro Tools, Sound Designer)

## Slide 2 — The Digital Revolution: DAW Software Evolution
- Early 1990s: Computer-based recording (Digidesign Pro Tools, Steinberg Cubase)
- Mid-1990s: Introduction of software synthesizers and samplers
- Late 1990s: Loop-based music creation (FruityLoops, later FL Studio)
- 2000-2002: Propellerhead Software releases Reason v1.0 (2001), ReBirth (1997-2005)
